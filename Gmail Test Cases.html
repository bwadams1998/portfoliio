Test Case 6: Gmail Login - Valid Credentials

Test ID: GMAIL-TC-001
Priority: High
Module: Authentication

What you need first:

  • A valid Gmail account
  • Be on the Gmail login page

Steps to test:

  1. Go to gmail.com
  2. Enter your email address
  3. Click "Next"
  4. Enter your password
  5. Click "Next" again

What should happen:

  • You get logged in
  • Your inbox loads up
  • You can see how many emails you have
  • The navigation menu is visible (Inbox, Sent, Drafts, etc.)
  • Your profile picture or initial appears in the top right

Test Data:

  • Email: testuser@gmail.com
  • Password: SecurePass123!

Test Case 7: Compose and Send Email

Test ID: GMAIL-TC-002
Priority: High
Module: Email Composition

What you need first:

  • Be logged into Gmail
  • Be in your inbox

Steps to test:

  1. Click the "Compose" button
  2. Type in the recipient's email in the "To" field
  3. Add a subject line
  4. Write your message in the body
  5. Hit "Send"

What should happen:

  • The compose window pops up
  • You can type in all the fields
  • The email sends successfully
  • You get a quick "Message sent" confirmation
  • The email shows up in your "Sent" folder
  • The compose window closes on its own

Test Data:

  • To: recipient@example.com
  • Subject: Test Email
  • Body: This is a test message.

Test Case 8: Search Emails by Keyword

Test ID: GMAIL-TC-003
Priority: Medium
Module: Search

What you need first:

  • Be logged in
  • Have some emails in your inbox

Steps to test:

  1. Find the search bar at the top
  2. Type in a keyword (like "invoice")
  3. Press Enter or click the search icon
  4. Look through the results

What should happen:

  • Search happens really fast (within a second or two)
  • You see emails that contain your keyword
  • The search term is highlighted in the results
  • Each result shows the sender, subject, date, and a preview
  • You can further filter by date, sender, attachments, etc.

Test Data:

  • Search Term: "invoice"

Test Case 9: Attach File to Email

Test ID: GMAIL-TC-004
Priority: Medium
Module: Email Composition

What you need first:

  • Be logged in
  • Have the compose window open

Steps to test:

  1. Click "Compose"
  2. Click the attachment icon (paperclip)
  3. Select "Attach files"
  4. Pick a file from your computer (under 25MB)
  5. Wait for it to upload
  6. Add a recipient and send the email

What should happen:

  • Your file browser opens up
  • The file uploads without issues
  • You can see a progress bar as it uploads
  • The file name and size appear in the compose window
  • The email sends with the attachment included
  • Gmail accepts files under the 25MB limit

Test Data:

  • File: TestDocument.pdf (2MB)

Test Case 10: Mark Email as Spam

Test ID: GMAIL-TC-005
Priority: Medium
Module: Email Management

What you need first:

  • Be logged in
  • Have at least one email in your inbox

Steps to test:

  1. Open or select an email
  2. Click the "Report spam" icon or button
  3. See what happens to the email

What should happen:

  • The email immediately goes to your Spam folder
  • You get a confirmation message
  • It disappears from your inbox
  • You briefly see an "Undo" option in case you made a mistake
  • Future emails from that sender might get automatically filtered

Test Data:

  • Email: Any email in inbox
